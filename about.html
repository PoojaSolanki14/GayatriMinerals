Welcome to
Gayatri Minerals

Gayatri Minerals was established in the year 1989 and deals in Manufacturing and supplying Whiting Chalk Power of unique and unmatched quality. We have been focusing on the quality control according to the ISO Standards and Customer's need.

The processing unit is spread over the area of 22,000 sq ft, and the unit has its own mining areas around Porbandar, the only place in Asia yielding Whiting Chalk Powder. We assure our customers moist-free product throughout and our mission is to exceed our customer's expectations by providing them with the best quality.

Our monthly production ranges from 1000 – 1200 metric tones per month.

After Mining Slurry, Chalk Lumps (Raw Material) are formed. Later on it is transported to Manufacturing Unit and it is pulverized and transformed into the final product, WHITING CHALK POWDER.

The product is currently being supplied all over India and also exported to Asian Countries.
